About Me

Connor Boi Pigeon is a 27-year-old photographer who enjoys theatre, charity work and jigsaw puzzles. He is loveable and generous, but can also be very stingy and a bit cowardly. He is a Swedish Christian who defines himself as straight. He has a degree. He is allergic to peanuts. Physically, Connor is in good shape. He is tall with fair skin, brown hair and brown eyes. He grew up in a middle class neighbourhood. He was raised by his mother, his father having left when he was young. He is currently married to Viola Paula Long. Viola is 9 years older than him and works as a sales assistant. Connor has two children with wife Viola: Stefan aged 0 and Felix aged 1. Connor's best friend is an intelligence researcher called Anton Stewart. They have a very firey friendship. He also hangs around with Jan Cook and Felicia Campbell. They enjoy watching YouTube videos together.
